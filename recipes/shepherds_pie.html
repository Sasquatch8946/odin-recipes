Shepherd's Pie

Description

Shepherd's Pie is a meat and vegetable casserole topped with mashed potatoes that are baked to a crisp. The dish originated in either Ireland or England some time in the 18th century. Technically, "Shepherd's pie" is made with ground lamb or mutton, whereas the version of the dish made with ground beef is "cottage pie."

Ingredients

MEAT FILLING:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 lb. ground beef or lamb
  • 2 tsp parsley
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup frozen mixed peas & carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn kernels

POTATO TOPPING:

  • 2 lb. russet potatoes
  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup half & half or heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

MAKE THE MEAT FILLING.

  1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Add onions. Cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add ground beef or lamb and break a part. Add parsley, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook 6-8 until meat is browned.
  3. Add Worcestershire sauce and garlic and stir. Cook 1 minute.
